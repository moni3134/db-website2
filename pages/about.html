Who We Are

Engineering Recruitment with Purpose

David Blackwell Recruitment was built on a simple belief: engineering businesses deserve a recruiter who actually understands engineering.

We specialise exclusively in engineering talent — from welding and fabrication to CNC, mechanical, electrical, and maintenance. That focus means we know our candidates, we know our clients, and we get it right first time.

Based in the Midlands with coverage across the UK, we work with engineering companies of all sizes — from growing SMEs to established manufacturers.
